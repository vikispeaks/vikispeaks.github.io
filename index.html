About Me

I’m a Responsive UI Developer and Hybrid Mobile Application Developer living in Chennai, India. With a background in Computer Science and Web Development, I create digital experiences that are exciting, innovative, elegant and most important — effective. Whether it’s for desktop, mobile or tablet, the things I engineer will always reach the customer wherever they are, when they’re most interested.

I am pursuing a dream of a Career developing Responsive Web Apps and Mobile Web Apps using Restful Web-services and HTML5 integrated components for the same. I am involved in Flash to HTML5 conversions of various Webpages and Games in particular proving ample Openness in device and OS specifications. I spent a year nurturing myself and the Team in the Research & Development side of Digital Initatives at Cognizant, working and creating web applications for Fortune 500 clients.

Currently, you can find me Engineering Hybrid Mobile applications and Developing Responsive Layouts at Tata Consultancy Services in Chennai, India.
